Solution Details

Solution Features

Basic Features

  • Ability to sign up and login via social media account or email/password.
  • Ability to upload job sheet data (csv) and associated schematic to the cloud.
  • Ability to sync job sheet data and schematic pdf to iOS client for offline usage.
  • Ability to take pictures for each component and annotate each picture. (iOS)
  • Ability to sync any changes made to the job sheet data and upload images and annotations to the cloud.
  • Ability to download component pictures with annotations from the cloud through the web/iOS client.

Advanced Features

  • Ability to store revision history of job sheet data
  • Edit the parts and export to a new csv file
  • Auto-fill (a part that already exists in the database when editing jobsheet)

User modes

User

  • Access web client to upload schematics and job sheet data (csv file)
  • can upload photos and annotate them (possibly - not required right away)
  • Access iOS client to capture and annotate photos.
  • can edit photo before uploaded to storage, but after upload is complete cannot edit photo without admin permissions
  • allows for user to pickup follow on jobs once completed their initial job of the day

Admin

  • Assign jobs to users within that specific organization
  • Power to delete photos once uploaded
  • Power to change the schematic provided
